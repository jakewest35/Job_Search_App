Welcome to the Job Search Aggrigator

I created this website as a way to aggregate job postings from my 2 most utilized job hunting websites, LinkedIn and Indeed. This website utilizes the LinkedIn and Indeed API's made available through RapidAPI.com to gather job postings based on the (limited) search parameters made available through the API's. This experience was my first time utilizing API's through a django-based web project.
